About us

The mission of our website is to allow for a more convenient access of locating used textbooks for students, and help to pair the buyers with the sellers. The advantages of using our website is that students can save money and stay within a reasonable budget when it comes to purchasing required books or resources for their courses. Usually, used textbooks can be bought for a fraction of the price, compared to buying a brand-new book from the Laurier Bookstore. The site can also be used by students looking to make some money from old textbooks that they no longer need. The website will be designed to provide a more convenient and organized access for the searching of their desired textbooks, and allow for a prompt response between the buyers and sellers.
